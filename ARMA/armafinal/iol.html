Key Features & Benefits

Engineered for precision. Designed for better outcomes.

Seamless Vision Performance

  • Light distribution: 60% distance, 15% intermediate, and 25% near vision ensures optimal clarity.

  • No diffractive rings or zones on the optical surface, minimizing visual disturbances.

Enhanced Patient Comfort

  • Total diameter is 13mm and optic area is 7.5mm. The 7.5mm optic area is designed to reduce glare and halos in night-time or mesopic conditions.

  • Zig-zag haptics provide superior stability and integration within the capsular bag.

Ease of Placement

  • Placement in the capsular bag at a precise temporal or 0–180° position is mandatory for optimal outcomes.

  • The unique loading system and cartridge enable quick, smooth, and consistent lens injection for hassle-free implantation.

Material Excellence

  • Manufactured using raw materials from Contamac, UK, ensuring premium quality and durability.

  • The 360° square edge design minimizes the chances of PCO (Posterior Capsule Opacification).

Clinical Considerations

Suitable for a wide range of patients.

Exclusion criteria: Cases with significant diopter of cylinder power or fundus abnormalities.

Recommendation for Best Results

To achieve optimal vision for near, intermediate, and distance, always start with the least minus power during selection. With Strident or Spirant Autofocus Pro, you can ensure unmatched visual clarity, hassle-free implantation (Strident Preloaded Injector), and superior patient satisfaction.
