Privacy Policy

At S. Lashkaria Group, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle your information with the highest standards of transparency and security.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, contact number, and email address when you inquire about a property, download a brochure, or visit our site. This information is gathered solely to provide you with tailored real estate solutions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us personalize your property search, provide project updates, and improve our digital services to offer a better browsing experience. We use these insights to match you with developments that fit your lifestyle and investment goals.

3. Data Protection

We implement advanced encryption and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our digital infrastructure is regularly audited to ensure the highest safety standards for our clients.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with trusted service partners (like banking or legal consultants) necessary to facilitate your property purchase, ensuring a seamless transition from inquiry to ownership.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to analyze traffic and remember your preferences. These small files help us understand how you interact with our listings, allowing us to refine our interface. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. We believe in your right to control your information. Contact our compliance team at any time to exercise these rights or request a data audit.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries, reach out to us via email or call our dedicated support line. Our team is here to ensure your data journey with S. Lashkaria Group is secure and transparent.
